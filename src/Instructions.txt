
Welcome to the tutorial here you will learn how to play the game!

1. Choose a pet of our amazing of selection: dog, duck, cat, and dragon then name it!!!
2. After creating a pet you will enter the world of EvoPet! 
3. Flip between the screens: Kitchen, Bedroom, Playground, and Vet, to then preform actions like, feed, sleep, play or take your pet to the doctor when his health is getting low.
4. The objective is to evolve your pet to the max level. Through the increase of your score and coin collection. Your pet will evolve when you reach 1000 and 2000 in score! There are 3 total levels for each animal giving you lots to explore!
5. Coins and score will increase over time but make sure to use your coins effectively on your necessities to keep your pet alive!
6. Make sure to keep your pet healthy and alive by keeping track of its stats and doing the according actions. For example when your pet is hungry make sure to go to the kitchen and feed it!
7. STAY ALIVE and HAVE FUN!!!
